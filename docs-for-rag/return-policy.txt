Jakarta Book Store - Return Policy

Return Eligibility
-----------------
1. Physical Books
   - 30-day return window from delivery date
   - Must be in original condition
   - No visible damage or markings
   - Original packaging preferred
   - Receipt or order number required

2. Digital Products
   - 7-day return window
   - Must not be downloaded or accessed
   - One-time refund policy
   - Account access revoked upon refund
   - System verification required

3. Damaged or Defective Items
   - Report within 48 hours of delivery
   - Photo documentation required
   - Free return shipping provided
   - Immediate replacement option
   - Full refund available

Return Process
-------------
1. Initiating Returns
   - Log into your account
   - Select order for return
   - Choose return reason
   - Print return label (if applicable)
   - Package item securely

2. Return Shipping
   - Free returns for defective items
   - Customer pays return shipping for non-defective items
   - Premium members get free returns
   - Tracking provided for all returns
   - Insurance included

3. Processing Time
   - 1-2 business days for inspection
   - 3-5 business days for refund
   - Email notifications at each step
   - Expedited processing for Premium members

Refund Information
-----------------
1. Payment Methods
   - Original payment method refunded
   - Store credit option available
   - Additional 5% bonus for store credit
   - Gift card purchases refunded as store credit
   - International orders may have currency conversion fees

2. Refund Timeline
   - Digital products: 2-3 business days
   - Physical books: 5-7 business days
   - Bank processing time additional
   - Status updates via email
   - Online tracking available

Non-Returnable Items
-------------------
1. Special Cases
   - Custom orders
   - Bulk purchases
   - Sale items marked "Final Sale"
   - Used or damaged books
   - Activated digital content

2. Exceptions
   - Defective items always accepted
   - Wrong items shipped
   - Missing components
   - Damaged during shipping

Exchange Policy
-------------
1. Direct Exchange
   - Available for same title
   - Different format available
   - Shipping costs may apply
   - Must be same price point
   - Subject to availability

2. Price Differences
   - Price difference charged/refunded
   - Current prices apply
   - Promotions may not apply
   - Gift card balance adjustment
   - Store credit for difference

Contact for Returns
------------------
- Email: returns@jakartabookstore.com
- Phone: 1-800-JAKARTA (Mon-Fri, 9AM-5PM EST)
- Chat: Available 24/7 through website
- In-person support not available

Special Circumstances
-------------------
1. Bulk Returns
   - Special handling required
   - Contact customer service first
   - Custom return labels provided
   - Volume restrictions may apply
   - Special pricing adjustments

2. International Returns
   - Different policies may apply
   - Customs fees non-refundable
   - Extended processing time
   - Documentation requirements vary
   - Contact support for assistance
