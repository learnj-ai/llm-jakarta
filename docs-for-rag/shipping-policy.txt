Jakarta Book Store - Shipping Policy

Shipping Methods
---------------
1. Domestic Shipping (United States)
   - Standard Shipping
     * Delivery time: 5-7 business days
     * Cost: $4.99 for orders under $75
     * Free for orders over $75
   
   - Express Shipping
     * Delivery time: 2-3 business days
     * Cost: $12.99
     * Free for Premium Members

   - Next Day Delivery
     * Available for orders placed before 2 PM EST
     * Cost: $24.99
     * Available in continental US only

2. International Shipping
   - Standard International
     * Delivery time: 10-15 business days
     * Cost: Starting at $19.99
     * Varies by destination country

   - Express International
     * Delivery time: 5-7 business days
     * Cost: Starting at $39.99
     * Tracking included

Order Processing
---------------
1. Processing Times
   - Orders processed within 24 hours
   - Weekend orders processed next business day
   - Holiday schedules posted on website
   - Bulk orders may require additional processing time

2. Tracking Information
   - Provided via email once order ships
   - Real-time tracking updates
   - SMS notifications available
   - Track through account dashboard

Special Conditions
-----------------
1. Multiple Book Orders
   - May be shipped in separate packages
   - No additional shipping charge
   - Individual tracking numbers provided
   - Combined shipping rates applied

2. Digital Products
   - Instant delivery
   - Download link via email
   - Available in account library
   - No shipping charges apply

3. Pre-orders
   - Charged at time of shipping
   - Release date estimates provided
   - Priority processing
   - Free shipping on pre-orders over $50

Shipping Restrictions
--------------------
1. Address Requirements
   - Must be valid street address
   - No P.O. boxes for express shipping
   - Military addresses accepted
   - Address verification required

2. International Restrictions
   - Import duties responsibility of customer
   - Some countries may have restrictions
   - Additional documentation may be required
   - Customs delays possible

Insurance and Loss Protection
---------------------------
1. Package Insurance
   - All orders automatically insured
   - Coverage up to $100 included
   - Additional insurance available
   - Claims process assistance provided

2. Lost Packages
   - Investigation initiated after 14 days domestic
   - 30 days international
   - Replacement or refund provided
   - Tracking required for claims
